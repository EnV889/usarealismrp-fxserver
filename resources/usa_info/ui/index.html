Character Appearance

You can change your player model at any clothing store marked on your map. You can further customize your player model at a barber shop. You can get tattoos from a tattoo shop. You can get bracelets and watches at Vangelico.

HUD

The two bars under your health (green) and armor (blue) bars indicate your food (yellow) and thirst (purple) levels. Make sure to buy yourself food and drink from somewhere so you don't pass out from dehydration or hunger! Your money on hand can be seen using /cash or /wallet. You can see how much you have in the bank by typing /bank into chat or by visiting a bank and pressing "E" to open the menu.

Getting a Job

There are various ways to earn money. One way is to try to flex your entrepreneurial skills by starting a business or organization of your own. Another way could be to find a business that is hiring. You can go to any of the icons on the map such as Bubba's Tow Co., Downtown Taxi Co., GoPostal, etc and sign in or begin doing the job to earn money. There are also illegal ways to earn money but they are not marked on the map and you will have ask people (in character) how to access them.

Getting a phone

Go to a "general store" marked on your map and purchase a cell phone. You can then open and use the phone by using the F1 hotkey or doing M > Inventory > Cell Phone > Use.

Inventory Tips

To use, trade, and drop items in your inventory, do M > Inventory and then right click on the item to use or trade.


The first four slots (from the top left) of your inventory correspond to your 1, 2, 3, and 4 keyboard keys. This replaces the default GTA weapon wheel selection method. You can use TAB to store your currently selected weapon. You must have your GTA "weapon select" settings set as default values for this to work as expected.


Weapons

Firearm permits, weapons, and weapon customizations are available at Gun Stores. There are illegal customizations (suppressors, extended magazines, etc) available at unmarked locations around the map. Please be responsible in your use of a firearm. Do not be trigger happy. Limit your use of it.

Cinematic Mode

You can press F5 to enter cinematic mode. This is great for immersive RP moments!
